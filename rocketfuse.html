Rocketfuse

The first Rockethands game, for iPhone and iPod touch.

Guide a marooned spaceship out of a series of caves using thrusters. You must place them carefully as they will ignite on a timer and propel the ship around according to their size.

Rocketfuse has a charming cardboard asthetic and a lively original soundtrack.
